/* WHAT IS THIS? */

DoubleTrack Browser is an experimental browser extension exploring
unconventional privacy protection through deliberate noise generation.

Instead of hiding your data, it creates believable fictional browsing
patterns to obscure real activity from tracking systems.

Philosophy: "Visibility as camouflage in the age of surveillance capitalism"
