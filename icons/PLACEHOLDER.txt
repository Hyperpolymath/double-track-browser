This directory needs icon files in the following sizes:
- icon16.png
- icon32.png
- icon48.png
- icon128.png

Use the icon.svg as a template and convert to PNG using:
- Online converter (e.g., cloudconvert.com)
- ImageMagick: convert -background none -resize 128x128 icon.svg icon128.png
- Inkscape: inkscape -w 128 -h 128 icon.svg -o icon128.png

Or design proper icons in Figma/Illustrator following the brand guidelines.
